Engr. (Mrs) Fausat Abiola Dare-Bello (MNSE)

Chairman

Engr. (Mrs.) Fausat Abiola Dare Bello Graduated from Federal University of Technology Minna, with Bachelor of Civil Engineering (B.ENG Civil) in the year 2000. Biola, as she is fondly called is Chartered with Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN), an active member of Nigerian Society of Engineers (NSE) and the Association of Professional Women Engineers (APWEN) with various Meritorious service Awards in recognition of her outstanding performances. She is also a member of Society for Women Engineers (SWE) USA, International Network of Women Engineers and Scientist (INWES).

Currently, she is a Chief Civil Engineer with Department of Development Control, Abuja Metropolitan Management Council (AMMC), Federal Capital Territory Administration (FCTA) in Abuja. Engr. Abiola hailed from the ancient township of Offa in Offa local government area of kwara state. She was born on the 6th January and happily married to Mr. Abdul Azeez Bello.

Engr. Charity Joseph Ojobo (MNSE)

Vice Chairman

Engr. Charity Joseph-Ojobo (nee Anazodo) is the first among five Children to Late Mr. Nathan and Chief Dr. Mrs. Joy Anazodo of Otolo Nnewi in Nnewi North L.G.A of Anambra State. She is a year 2000 graduate of Civil Engineering from Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka, Anambra State. She became a corporate member of NSE in June 2005 and registered with COREN in September 2006.

Charity, a geometric design Engineer with Reynolds Construction Company Nig. Ltd (R.C.C) relocated to Abuja in year 2010 and identified with this honourable group – APWEN Abuja. Her commitment created avenue for her to serve as the General Secretary from year 2011 – 2013, Treasurer from year 2013 – 2015 and Financial Secretary from year 2015 till date. Charity is happily married to Hon. Ojobo Joseph Hyacinth from Enugu State.
